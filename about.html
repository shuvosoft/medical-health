Dr. K Health & Wellness

Dr.K is a Frontline Professional that's eager to transform the thinking of the masses. Dr. K says;

Promote awareness, provide education and screenings to prevent as well as decrease preventive diseases. Through healthier lifestyles, regular healthcare visits, and health screenings (i.e.) breast exams, mammograms, colonoscopy, osteoporosis, skin assessment, eye exam, dental exam). Increase awareness of available access to local healthcare clinics, hospitals, wellness & beauty, exercise, fitness, and other resources.
